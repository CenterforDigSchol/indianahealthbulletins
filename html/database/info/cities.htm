Cities

    During the October 1899 - December 1910 time period, cities were divided into the following five classes, based on population:

    1. Cities with populations over 50,000
    2. Cities with populations from 25,000 to 50,000
    3. Cities with populations from 10,000 to 25,000
    4. Cities with populations from 5,000 to 10,000
    5. Cities with populations under 5,000
